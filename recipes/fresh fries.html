Home Description

These homemade French fries are perfectly crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. Seasoned with a touch of salt and pepper, they make the ultimate classic side dish for any meal!

Ingredients

  • 3 large Russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or enough for deep frying)
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash, peel, and slice the potatoes into even sticks.
  2. Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for about 30 minutes to remove excess starch, then dry them completely.
  3. Heat your oil in a deep skillet or pot to around 325°F and fry the potatoes for 5 minutes until soft but not browned, then remove them.
  4. Increase the oil heat to 375°F and fry them a second time for 2-3 minutes until completely crispy and golden brown.
  5. Drain on paper towels, toss with salt and pepper, and serve hot!
