Description

These are ultra-fluffy, golden-brown pancakes that are perfect for a cozy weekend breakfast. Serve them warm with a big pat of butter and plenty of maple syrup!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour and sugar together in a large bowl.
  2. Pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter, then mix until mostly smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour the batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
