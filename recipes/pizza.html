Description

This Pav Bhaji Pizza is the ultimate strret-style Indian Fusion recipes! It takes the spicy, buttery flavors of classic bhaji masala, spreads it across a pizza crust, and tops it witrh melted mozzarella, red onions, and fresh cilantro

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or ready-made pizza base
  • Thick, semi-dry Pav Bhaji masala
  • 4 oz fresh or shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped (for garnish)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (or heat a cast-iron skillet on medium-low if cooking on a tawa).
  2. Roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness and place it onto a greased pizza pan.
  3. Evenly spread a thick layer of the semi-dry pav bhaji masala over the base as your sauce.
  4. Add dollops of fresh mozzarella cheese and sprinkle a little cilantro on top.
  5. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
  6. Remove from the oven, garnish with chopped red onions and extra fresh cilantro, slice, and serve!
