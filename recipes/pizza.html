Description

This Pav Bhaji Pizza is the ultimate street-style Indian Fusion recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or ready-made pizza base
  • Thick, semi-dry Pav Bhaji masala
  • 4 oz fresh or shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F.
  2. Roll out your pizza dough to your desired thickness.
  3. Evenly spread a thick layer of the semi-dry pav bhaji masala over the base as your sauce.



  4. Add dollops of fresh mozzarella cheese and sprinkle a little cilantro on top.
  5. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven, garnish with chopped red onions, slice, and serve!
